Accommodation
Policy

For the convenience of the enthusiastic participants of Mood Indigo, we here in the Hospitality Department of MI take it upon ourselves to provide you with a convenient stay place within the Institute itself. Owing to the huge scale of participation, we have established a set of procedural guidelines to help make the process seamless and without glitches


Let's now walk you through them step wise


How To Avail

- Everybody willing to avail accommodation in Mood Indigo must register to get their MI numbers. You can register here.

- All participants have to contact the Contingent Leader (hereafter referred to as ‘CL’ for convenience) of their college; the details of whom would be made available to you once the CL is decided.

- The registration for Contingent Leader has started. You can register yourself at the CL portal.

- The CL will be given access to CL interface where they will be allowed to choose students from his/her college for availing accommodation for Mood Indigo. Last date of finalising the contingent would be near November

- CL will have to finalise the contingent before the given deadline and within the allotted contingent limit.


On arrival

- You must first head to the Accommodation Desk in the Student Activity Center, (fondly and famously known as ‘SAC’) along with your college ID card.

Your CL must have the list of his/her entire contingent with their MI numbers beforehand, which he/she has to produce at the desk.

- Each student will be given accommodation only after it has been confirmed by the Hospitality Core Group Members through the CL of the respective college

- After that, you will be allotted your place of stay, and will be provided with your accommodation booklet.


All About CL

- A Contingent Leader is selected from each and every college who will guide you through the accommodation process during your stay at IIT Bombay. He/She will bridge the gap between you and Mood Indigo. In order to be nominated as the CL for your college, you need an Official Letter on the letterhead of your college signed by the Director/Principal saying that you have been nominated as the Contingent Leader for Mood Indigo 2018. Along with this we may also need your other details including account details.

- Application form for being a CL of your college will be out soon. Please stay tune on our Fb page and website for further information.

- In addition to this, two other people are to be appointed as the Assistant CL. All three of you must mention your MI number along with your name in this letter.


Things to remember

- Owing to a large number of requests for accommodation, we are bound to provide it strictly on a shared basis.

- You will be given accommodation only after it has been confirmed by the Hospitality Core Group members of the festival, through the CL of your college.



Charges

- Online charges for accommodation : Rs 1800/- (Rs 100/- is refundable)
*For maximum of 5 days i.e. from 26th to 30th Dec, 2018 irrespective of duration of stay. Food is not included.

- In exceptional circumstances the refund amount may be forfeited subject to one or more of the following conditions:

  1. i) Permanent damage has been done to the room/dorm/accessories where accommodation has been provided.
  2. ii) Rooms are not vacated on or before 31st December 10:00 am.

- The payment will be done through the online portal whose link will be given to all the people allotted accommodation in IIT Bombay campus

- Deadline for selecting contingent is in month of November and for online payment is last week of November.

- Refund has to be collected from accommodation desk, between 8am-10am, 31st December 9AM to 1PM. No refund would be provided after that. Mood Indigo is not liable to refund the amount in case of any mistake made by the person paying the amount

How to reach
